This app is a client for the SvitloBot system and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • The app does not require user registration or authentication.
  • No personal identifiers, contact details, or sensitive data are collected.
  • The app sends a technical request to a remote server approximately every 60 seconds, only if internet access is available and the device is connected to a charger. This information is used solely to determine the presence or absence of electricity.
  • No data is shared with third parties.
